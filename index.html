About the Project

The Circular Economy for Household Organic Waste (CE4HOW) project promotes sustainable household organic waste management in Kakamega Municipality through interventions that encourage segregation at source, safe collection, and reuse of organic waste in composting and biogas production.

Key Partners:

  • Practical Action
  • Regen Organics
  • Kakamega County

Campaign Message

"Taka ni Mali" - Waste is Wealth

Segregate organic waste at source and contribute to a circular economy that creates environmental and economic value for our community.
